EDUCATION JOURNEY ✎﹏


When I was 9 years old, I was tranfered to Sekolah Kebangsaan Kenering at the end of the years of 2012. Everything was going well. At first, I found a hard time to adjust to my new environment. This is because there was a boy who kept mocking me, but I'm glad I didn't feel sad or down at that time. It could've effect my mental health. But as you know, back then, girls and boys don't get along well. So, we had like a wall between the gender. Eventually, we all be friends and suprisingly we get along well. I took my UPSR exam there as well and my results were quiet good. I really did study well at that time.

My highschool life began after a month break. I went to Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Kenering. The school was near to my house and I just have to walk to my school everyday. It took me like 5 minutes to go to school. Highschool was different from primary school, the subject were a bit intense than it would normally. I found it hard to catch up with the subject. Not all but MATHEMATIC! (T-T). I really hated that subject, so I did not do well in every examination. I only got pass in math. But it is what it is. For my PT3 exam, my results was not that impressive. I did not failed and that was more important to me at that time. Then after that I had to study hard for my SPM examination. This exam was very important for me wheiter I continued my study further or...maybe I have to find job afterwards, who knows right. So, I study hard for this exam and my results was...SUPRISINGLY GOOD. When I check my result that day, my eyes shed tears as I told my parents. I'm so grateful because my hard work paid off. I actually did not expect such result because my batch were having difficulties to attend school cause of Covid-19. Those reaction was me felt disbelieve and grateful.

Now, I have entered my university life, which at UiTM Merbok. I'm in the senior year already. I couldn't believe it, it just happened with a flash of my eyes. I would say university life is a bit hard, because I have to be independent and do things alone. But I'm glad I have my friends along by my side. Which are adi, sya and daus, I love them so much <3.They help me a lot in this journey. Sadly we'll be apart after our diploma end. So, we want end this diploma together and not leave anyone behind. In order to achieve that, we have been working hard in the past few days to finished this assignment and others. I hope we will get the best results at the end of this journey and this is not the last stop. I will continue to work hard in order to achieve bright future. Inshallah (૭ ｡•̀ ᵕ •́｡ )૭
